Deep Sea Mysteries

Fascinating facts about the extreme environment these creatures call home

Pressure Extreme

The pressure in the deepest trenches reaches over 1,000 atmospheres - enough to crush most submarines.

Perpetual Darkness

Below 1,000 meters, sunlight disappears completely, creating a world of eternal night.

Cold as Ice

Temperatures hover just above freezing, between 1-4°C (34-39°F) in most deep ocean zones.

Slow Metabolism

Many deep-sea creatures live extremely long lives with very slow metabolisms to conserve energy.

Bioluminescence

90% of deep-sea creatures produce light, the most common form of communication in the dark.

Unexplored Frontier

More than 80% of our ocean remains unmapped, unobserved, and unexplored.
